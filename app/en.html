Learn JavaScript

NodeSchool is for people totally new to programming, as well as experienced programmers looking to try a new language or skillset. Knowing some JavaScript will help, but is not necessary.

Self-Directed

NodeSchool workshops are self-guided. No boring lectures. All challenges are open source and are available online at nodeschool.io. This means that you can practice anytime.

For Everyone

We think that learning to program is great and everyone should feel welcome at our event. This includes our expectation that all participants be kind to each other and respect our Code of Conduct.

What is Nodeschool ? What is it about?


Nodeschool Costa Rica is part of the global effort to teach voluntarily Node.js and JavaScript all through classroom workshops using interactive modules that help you learn or improve writing code and solving problems with the help of mentors.

The source code of the workshops is completely open source and the effort is entirely driven by the community. And the best part is it's free!

Nodeschool events have been performed worldwide , successfully introducing beginners to JavaScript , Node.js and the command line . Interactive workshops can also be done at home, but attending the event in person will give you the opportunity to learn in an environment of integration and collaboration; And you can also help other beginners if you already have experience.

More information about the international community at  nodeschool.io

Nodeschool International Day #nodeschool-day


Each year we celebrate the international day of Nodeschool in which cities with nodeschool chapters organize workshops to learn Node.js and JavaScript and also networking, sharing the expirience through social media, streaming video and chat. The event is held simultaneously in different locations around the world including San Francisco , London , New York , Moscow, Tel Aviv , Paris , Lisbon, San Diego , Los Angeles , Austin and San Jose among others.
